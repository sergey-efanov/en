And here are some ideas and principles of creative thinking that might come in handy for you when working with the tracker:

Ideas generation: Don't restrict yourself with immediate evaluation and selection; give ideas the chance to develop.

Associations and connections: Look for connections between different ideas, objects, or concepts. Combine and unite different elements to create new ideas and solutions.

Think outside the box: Use different approaches to problem-solving. Try to look at things from unexpected perspectives and search for alternative paths to solutions.

Prototyping and testing: Don't strive for perfection right away. Create prototypes or models of your ideas to assess their viability. Testing will help you understand what works and what can be improved.

Collaboration and idea exchange: Work in a team or engage with other creative individuals. Exchanging ideas and opinions can lead to new insights and perspectives.

Learn to embrace mistakes: Creativity requires experimentation, and sometimes ideas may not work right away. But don't despair, don't be afraid to make mistakes, and learn from failures. It's part of the process of growth and development.
